About MDi Institute

Muhammad Faisal

Founder & Director

Our Story

MDi Institute was founded in rural Pakistan with a vision to bridge the technology education gap. As a BS Software Engineering student at Islamia College Peshawar, I recognized the need for accessible, high-quality education in computer science and AI for youth in underserved communities.

We believe in life-focused education that prepares students for both professional success and meaningful contributions to society. Our unique approach combines technical excellence with Islamic values and social responsibility.

Expertise: Python, AI, Machine Learning, Web Development, Ethical Tech

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth with technological skills combined with ethical values, creating leaders who can drive positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every young person, regardless of background, has access to quality education that prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Unique Approach

Technical Excellence

Cutting-edge courses in AI, programming, and computer science with hands-on projects.

Character Building

Integrating Islamic values and ethics into our curriculum to develop responsible tech leaders.

Community Focus

Podcasts, seminars, and mentorship programs to guide students beyond the classroom.
