Our Mission

MDi Institute was founded with a vision to bridge the technology education gap in rural Pakistan. We provide cutting-edge training in AI and computer science while instilling Islamic values and social responsibility in our students.

"I am Muhammad Faisal, a BS Software Engineering student at Islamia College Peshawar. I founded MDi Institute to empower youth with technological skills combined with ethical values. We believe in life-focused education that prepares students for both professional success and meaningful contributions to society."
